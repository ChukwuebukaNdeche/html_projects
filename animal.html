Animals and thier description

NAME
Tiger (panthera tigris)
COLOUR
Tigers are recognisable for its dark vertical stripe with on orange fur with a white underside.
BEST MEAL
This apex preditor primarily hunt large ungulate, such as wild boar and dear, but are also known to consume monkeys, buffalo, sloth bears, leopards and even crocodiles. They may also feed on domestic animals such as goats or cattle.
ANIMAL TYPE
Wild
ABILITIES
The tiger's adaptations of having noctural habits, striped camouflage, execellent vision, and hearing, sharp teeth and claws, a flexible spine and the ability to quietly and quickly pounce on a predator are the tiger's biggest advantage to remaining alive on our planet.
NAME
Hedgehog
COLOUR
Quill colour: Banding is average-wide and is tan or very light pale browncolour Body colour: Skin is typically nude or white and belly may be mottled tan or pale brown.
BEST MEAL
Hedgehog are omnivorous. They feed on insects, snails, frogs and toads, snakes, bird eggs, carricon, mushroom, grassroots, berries, melons and watermelons.
ANIMAL TYPE
Wild
ABILITIES
They have the ability to camouflage themselves from predators. They do this by licking certain objects or food until a frothy saliva forms, then rubbing the saliva onto thier skin and spines, which is believed to make a smell less palatable to predators.
NAME
Mole
COLOUR
Moles have short usually black velvety fur, with spade-like forelimbs with large claws that face toward the rear of the animal. pink fleshy snout and tiny eyes.
BEST MEAL
Their favorite meal includes earthworms, grubs, snails, spiders, small animals, and other insect.
ANIMAL TYPE
Wild
ABILITIES
Moles are highly specialised digging machines. They have a long tubular body, no external ears, and thier limbs are close to thier body. They have broad, shovel-like forelimbs that allows them to power through soil. They have very small eyes that is only good for light detection.
NAME
Walrus
COLOUR
Generally walrus are cinnamon brown overall. walrus appear quite pale in the water, after a sustained period in very cold water, they may appear almost white.
BEST MEAL
Walruses prefer molluscs - mainly bivalves such as clams. They also eat many other kinds of benthic invertebrates including worms, gastropods, cephalopods, crustaceans, sea cucumbers, and other soft-bodied animals. Walruses may occasionally prey on fishes such as polar cod.
ANIMAL TYPE
Wild
ABILITIES
Walruses use their tusks to haul themselves out of the water and onto the sea ice. Their tusks are also use for keeping breathing holes open in the ice, fighting with other walruses and for defence against predators.
NAME
Porcupine
COLOUR
Their fur ranges in colour from brownish-yellow to black, and they spot white highlighting in their quills.
BEST MEAL
Their diet changes seasonally. In the winter,they primarily eat evergreen needles and the inner bark of trees, often feeding heavily on a single tree causing damage or death to the tree. In the spring and summer, porcupine shift to eating berries,seeds, grasses, leaves,roots and stems.
ANIMAL TYPE
Wild
ABILITIES
A porcupine can remove other porcupine's quills by grasping them with their incisor and using its front feet. The porcupine's eye sight is poor, but it has an execellent sense of smell and hearing. Like other rodent, it has two large front teeth that grow continuously thoughout its life
NAME
Fox
COLOURr
They are four maim colour phases(or "morphs") found in the wild population: Red, Silver, Cross and White.
BEST MEAL
They are expert hunters, catching rabbits, rodents, birds, frogs, and earthworm as well as eating carricon. They are omnivores as they dine on berries too.
ANIMAL TYPE
Wild
ABILITIES
Their huntig skills are aided by stealthy senses of smell, hearing and night vision. Red foxes can hear the low frequency sound of small animals digging under the ground, leaves, or snow. They use the earth's magnetic field to help zero on their prey before pouncing.

A wombat's diet can consist of coarses native grasses, sedges, rushes, succulent plant roots and tubers and pasture grasses.

NAME
Lynxes
COLOUR
Their body colour ranges from medium brown to goldish to beige white. All species of lynx have white fur on Their chest, bellies and on their legs. The lynx colouring, fur lenght and paw size vary according to the climate.
ANIMAL TYPE
Wild
ABILITIES
Lynx climb and swim well and can make great leaps. They like to sneak up on their prey rather than chase it down. Lynx are generally silent, execept during mating season. They are close related to bob cat and caracal.
NAME
Hornbill
COLOUR
They plumage of hornbill is typically black, grey, white, or brown, and is frequently offset by bright colours on their bill, or by patches of bare coloured skin on the face or wattles
BEST MEAL
Hornbill primarily eat fat-rich and sugar-rich fruits, but they also feed on small mammals, birds, eggs, amphibians, reptiles and insects as well as figs.
ANIMAL TYPE
Wild
ABILITIES
The hornbill is an omnivorous bird that feast almost exclusively on fruits, insects or other small animals. The bill has tha ability to pluck fruit from the trees, and the end of the bill has sharp notches for tearing apart food. Their bodies are also very efficient at extracting limited protein from fruits.
NAME
Cormorant
COLOUR
Adults are blackish overall with white throat and yellowish skin around the bill. When breeding, adults have a square patch of white on their thigh and white neck feathers, juveniles are brownish with a whitish throat and belly, with some brownish streaking at the edge.
BEST MEAL
Cormorant are opportunistic predator that solely consume fish. Virtually every species of shag or cormorant consumes fish and aquatic animals such as shrimp, crustaceans, and other shellfish, as well as the occassional octopus or squid.
ANIMAL TYPE
Wild
ABILITIES
Cormorants are expert divers. Some dive as deep as 45 meters(150 feet). They speed along underwater via their webbed feet, using their wings as rudders. Some colonies of Cormorants have been observed herding fish for more efficient hunting.
